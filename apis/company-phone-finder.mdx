title: Company Phone Finder API
description: Phone numbers create direct lines to prospects that email alone can't match. The Company Phone Finder API returns up to two verified business phone numbers from company names, domains, or LinkedIn URLs with 93% confidence scores. Built for developers building CRM enrichment and customer support tools, this RESTful endpoint delivers validated contact numbers that power outbound calling systems, data completion workflows, and multi-channel outreach campaigns across your sales stack.
