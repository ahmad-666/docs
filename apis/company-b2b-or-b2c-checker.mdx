Built for sales automation platforms, market segmentation tools, and lead scoring systems, this RESTful endpoint delivers business model classification data—including mixed-model indicators, vertical market focus, and customer acquisition signals—that power qualified lead routing, personalized messaging strategies, and TAM calculation workflows across your revenue intelligence stack.
