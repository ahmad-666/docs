description: Targeting precision depends on knowing whether companies sell to businesses or consumers before you allocate outreach resources. The Company B2B or B2C Checker API analyzes domain signals across CUFinder's 85M+ company database to classify whether a company operates a B2B or B2C business model, returning categorization labels with confidence scores and supporting indicators like customer type mentions, transaction patterns, and product positioning language. Built for sales automation platforms, market segmentation tools, and lead scoring systems, this RESTful endpoint delivers business model classification data—including mixed-model indicators, vertical market focus, and customer acquisition signals—that power qualified lead routing, personalized messaging strategies, and TAM calculation workflows across your revenue intelligence stack.
