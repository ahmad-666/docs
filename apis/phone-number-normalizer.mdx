---
title: Phone Number Normalizer API
description: Data quality issues start when phone numbers arrive in dozens of inconsistent formats across your contact database. The Phone Number Normalizer API standardizes phone numbers into E.164 international format, returning cleaned numbers with country codes, area codes, and validation status that ensure CRM integration compatibility and dialer system accuracy. Built for data enrichment platforms, contact management systems, and communication automation tools, this RESTful endpoint delivers normalized phone data—including format corrections, carrier type detection, and validity flags—that power click-to-dial functionality, SMS delivery optimization, and duplicate contact consolidation workflows across your sales and marketing stack.
