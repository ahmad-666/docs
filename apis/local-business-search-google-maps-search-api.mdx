title: Local Business Search API
description: Local businesses represent untapped markets that B2C and service-based companies overlook in traditional B2B databases. The Local Business Search API queries neighborhood businesses by location and industry—returning phones, emails, ratings, and social profiles—with 95% confidence scores. Built for developers creating local marketing and service provider platforms, this RESTful endpoint delivers comprehensive small business data including NAICS codes and Google Maps integration that power geo-targeted campaigns, competitor analysis, and community outreach workflows across your sales stack.
